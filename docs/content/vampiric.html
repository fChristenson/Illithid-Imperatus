Vampiric

Vampiric mind flayers, also known as vampiric illithids, were created when an illithid tadpole infected with vampirism entered a humanoid. They could not create spawn and needed both fresh blood (or other bodily liquids such as cerebrospinal fluid) and fresh brains to survive. Vampiric mind flayers were found in Bluetspur after the God-Brain, an elder brain ruling the area, was infected with vampirism, along with its tadpoles.
